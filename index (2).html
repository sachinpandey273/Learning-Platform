about us

18 years of experience

A virtual class is an online educational environment where teaching and learning occur through digital mediums. Utilizing platforms like Zoom or Google Meet, virtual classes feature live sessions, recorded lectures, and collaborative tools. Course materials are presented digitally, offering flexibility for students to access resources and attend classes from any location with an internet connection. Assessments are conducted online, and communication is facilitated through email, messaging, and discussion boards. Virtual classes provide a dynamic and accessible learning experience, accommodating diverse schedules and fostering engagement through multimedia content and interactive elements.
